What can we say about Barbarella, Queen of the Galaxy? Well ...

"BARBARELLA is a beautiful girl with long hair, golden skin and a strange sweet name. Clothes cramp her style. But somehow she remains mysterious. Fragile. Yet invincible. Above all, BARBARELLA maintains a sense of innocence. She is a wild creature, realistic, crafty, emancipated.

BARBARELLA is a champion Earth astronaut. She lives in a future century. She handles herself like a female James Bond, vanquishing evil in all forms and rewarding - in her own loving way - the men who befriend her in outer space. Whether she is wrestling with Black Guards - hollow leather robots - the evil queen of the perverted City of Sogo, or restoring the lost morale of her fine feathered angel friend, Pygar, BARBARELLA is in command of the situation. Even though, more often than not, she is underessed for action. Uninhibited ... Irresistible ... Female ... BARBARELLA is love. Love. All love ... " - From the Barbarella 1968 British Press Book
